## Section 2: What we've built

> 📝 Nội bộ: portfolio overview — 1 câu cho mỗi project, focus vào cái hay agent làm được.

**Not chatbots. Agents that think, decide, and act.**

### Relmo — real estate CRM

The agent predicts which client relationships are dying — before the sales rep even notices. It reads between the lines of WhatsApp conversations, scores relationship health, and silently suggests matching listings while you chat.

### Skin Agent — AI beauty advisor

The agent doesn't trust what users tell it. "I have oily skin" — 60% of the time, that's wrong. So instead of accepting it, the agent asks clinical counter-questions to catch misdiagnosis before recommending anything. And if symptoms suggest something serious, it refuses to play doctor.

### VisibleBrands — AIO audit

Can ChatGPT find your website? This agent crawls your site and scores how well AI assistants can understand, extract, and recommend your content. Think Lighthouse, but for the AI era.

### MemoryOS — enterprise knowledge

Your company's knowledge is scattered across Notion, Slack, and Google Drive. This agent finds the right answer from 10,000+ docs — and tells you how confident it is. Green, yellow, or red. If it's not sure, it says so. If the source is stale, it warns you.

### Cyberk Internal — our own AI ops

We eat our own cooking. Semantic search across all company knowledge, an AI mentor for onboarding, and process QA agents that catch mistakes before they ship.

## Section 3: How we build agents

> 📝 Nội bộ: framework section — giải thích approach, không liệt kê tech stack.

**Most AI projects fail not because of bad models — but because of bad engineering.**

Building an agent is more than plugging in an LLM API. We've developed a 5-layer framework, tested across 5 production projects:

**Layer 0 — Research first.**
We study your market and competitors before writing a single line of code. Most agencies skip this entirely.

**Layer 1 — Context engineering.**
Generic AI knows everything about nothing specific. We ingest, structure, and embed your business data so the agent understands YOUR world — not just the internet.

**Layer 2 — Agent workflow.**
Tools, APIs, payments, search, actions. We use template agents to cut delivery time by 40%.

**Layer 3 — Quality control.**
Anti-hallucination by design. Confidence scoring, source citations, safety guardrails. The agent doesn't guess — it knows, or it says it doesn't.

**Layer 4 — Production monitoring.**
We don't ship and walk away. Trace, telemetry, optimization. We watch the agent in production and keep improving it.

**Foundation: hybrid AI architecture.**
Small models for frequent, privacy-sensitive tasks. Large models for complex reasoning. The right tool for the right job — at a fraction of the cost.

## Section 4: Why Cyberk for AI

> 📝 Nội bộ: angle mới — Cyberk là AI-native company, không phải agency "cũng làm AI."

**We don't just build AI. We run on it.**
Every person at Cyberk works with AI every day. Our engineers write code with AI. Our knowledge base is searchable by AI. Our internal processes are monitored by AI agents we built ourselves. We're not an agency that added "AI" to the services page — we rebuilt our entire workflow around it.

When you work with us, you're working with people who already know where AI saves time, where it hallucinates, where it needs guardrails, and where it genuinely changes everything. That's not something you learn from tutorials — it's something you learn from living with it.

**We understand patterns, not just projects.**
AI advisors for skincare and AI advisors for real estate share the same underlying architecture: context ingestion, domain reasoning, confidence scoring, safety guardrails. The domain changes. The engineering patterns don't.

That means we can apply what we've learned to your industry — whether it's finance, education, logistics, legal, or something we haven't seen yet. The faster we understand your domain, the faster your agent ships.

**We tell you what you need to hear.**
One client wanted <2% hallucination. Industry best is 5–10%. We pushed back, proposed realistic targets, and built a confidence scoring system instead. We'd rather lose a deal than set you up for failure.

**AI isn't a feature you ship once.**
Most vendors deploy and walk away. We monitor agents in production — trace, telemetry, continuous tuning. AI systems drift. Models update. Data changes. If nobody's watching, quality degrades silently. We watch.
