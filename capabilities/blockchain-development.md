## Section 4: What we build with it

> 📝 Nội bộ: map vào nhu cầu cụ thể của khách — "cái tôi cần có trong list không?"

**If it lives on-chain, we've probably built it.**

### DeFi protocols

The money layer of blockchain. We've built the full spectrum:

- **DEX & AMM** — automated market makers, liquidity pools, swap engines
- **Lending & borrowing** — collateral management, interest rate models, liquidation engines
- **Staking & yield farming** — reward distribution, vesting mechanics, compounding strategies
- **Stablecoins** — pegged asset mechanisms, reserve management
- **Payment rails** — cross-border crypto payments, on/off ramps
→ _Example: Atlas Stablecoin — pegged asset infrastructure from smart contracts to frontend._

### NFT & digital assets

Beyond JPEGs — programmable digital ownership:

- **NFT marketplaces** — minting, trading, auctions, royalty enforcement
- **NFT collections** — generative art, PFPs, membership passes
- **Dynamic NFTs** — on-chain metadata evolution, game assets
- **Custom token standards** — ERC-721, ERC-1155, SPL tokens, and custom implementations
→ _Example: Humanity Protocol — NFT minting system for node sale licenses._
→ _Example: Nexium — AI-powered NFT generation and minting._

### RWA tokenization

Bringing real-world assets on-chain:

- **Asset fractionalization** — real estate, securities, commodities
- **Compliance frameworks** — KYC/AML integration, regulatory requirements
- **Secondary markets** — trading tokenized assets
→ _Example: Helix — end-to-end RWA tokenization platform for financial assets._

### DePIN (decentralized physical infrastructure)

The new frontier:

- **Node management dashboards** — registration, monitoring, rewards
- **Token distribution** — miner incentives, staking mechanics
→ _Example: Humanity DePIN Dashboard — centralized management for decentralized infrastructure nodes._

### DAO & governance

Decentralized decision-making infrastructure:

- **Governance contracts** — proposal creation, voting mechanics, execution
- **Treasury management** — multi-sig wallets, fund allocation
- **Token-weighted voting** — on-chain governance with delegation
→ _Example: Dao.lives — DAO infrastructure for community-driven projects._

### Token infrastructure

Everything your token needs to launch and thrive:

- **Token contracts** — ERC-20, SPL, custom standards with vesting, locking, and governance
- **Fair launch platforms** — transparent, trustless token distribution
- **Launchpad infrastructure** — IDO, IEO, and direct listing tools
→ _Example: Fair Launch — transparent token distribution platform._
