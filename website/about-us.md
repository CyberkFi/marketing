## Section 1: Four values, no rulebook

> 📝 Nội bộ: Tâm – Cầu – Nghệ – Tín. Giữ nguyên tinh thần, viết cho khách hàng international hiểu.

We don't have a 50-page employee handbook. We have a compass with four values. Every decision — from architecture choices to client conversations — runs through them.

**Tâm — heart in the product.**
Your mind goes where your heart is. We put our heart in the product itself — not in timesheets, not in process theatre. When an engineer cares about the product as deeply as the founder does, the result is different. You can feel it.

**Cầu — relentless curiosity.**
In tech, standing still is falling behind. We treat curiosity as a discipline, not a personality trait. Every engineer is expected to learn, challenge assumptions, and stay ahead of the technology they work with. ERC-8004 launched last month? We've already read the spec.

**Nghệ — craftsman's pride.**
*Nghệ* means craft. It's the refusal to ship something ugly, fragile, or lazy. It's the extra hour spent making the smart contract gas-efficient. The design system that actually scales. The code that the next developer can read without a decoder ring.

**Tín — trust, earned by results.**
Trust isn't built by promises. It's built by delivering what we said we'd deliver, when we said we'd deliver it. It's why clients come back, and why they refer their friends. Tín is the reason Cyberk exists after five years.

## Section 2: Master deeply, not broadly

> 📝 Nội bộ: Bí quyết 1 — "Chăm hay không bằng tay quen" + focus vào blockchain.

> *"I fear not the man who has practiced 10,000 kicks once, but I fear the man who has practiced one kick 10,000 times."* — Bruce Lee

Most agencies chase every trend. We made a different choice.

We said no to web2 projects. We said no to mobile-only shops. We said no to "we do everything" positioning. Instead, we went deep — **blockchain and AI, and nothing else** — for five years straight.

The trade-off was real:
- We turned away clients outside our domain.
- We stayed small on purpose.
- We accepted a narrower market.

What we got in return can't be copied:

| What we mastered | What it means for you |
| --- | --- |
| Every EVM chain + Solana, Aptos, Sui, Stellar | We don't need a learning curve on your chain |
| Every smart contract standard from ERC-20 to ERC-8183 | We pick the right standard, not the popular one |
| DeFi, NFT, RWA, DePIN, DAO — shipped to mainnet | We've seen your problem before |
| The Cyberk Framework — 40+ projects refined | Your project starts at mile 40, not mile zero |

## Section 3: The framework — unchanged principles, infinite adaptations

> 📝 Nội bộ: Bí quyết 2 — "Dĩ bất biến, ứng vạn biến" + Cyberk Framework.

Every blockchain project looks unique. A DEX is different from an NFT marketplace. A stablecoin is different from a DAO.

But look deeper and the patterns repeat: wallet connection, smart contract architecture, indexing, authentication, deployment pipelines, monitoring. The 80% that doesn't change — we've already built it.

We call this the **Cyberk framework**. It's not a product — it's the accumulated infrastructure from 40+ production projects:

```
Smart Contracts  → Audited templates for DeFi, NFT, token, DAO, RWA
Backend          → Pre-configured indexing, event processing, API scaffolding
Frontend         → Wallet connect, chart components, web3-specific UI patterns
Infrastructure   → CI/CD, monitoring, auto-scaling from 0 to 100,000 users overnight
Documents        → Architecture templates, PRD/SRS structure, deployment runbooks
```

When a client brings a new DEX idea, we don't start from scratch. We deploy the framework in minutes. Then we spend 100% of our effort on the features that make their product unique.

**The result:**
- Projects that used to take 3 months ship in 30 days.
- Code quality is consistent — because the foundation was tested across 40 projects.
- Scaling is built-in — our infrastructure handles 0 to 100,000 users without redesign.

Every project we ship makes the framework stronger. Every client benefits from the clients who came before.

## Section 4: Show, don't tell — every single day

> 📝 Nội bộ: Bí quyết 3 — "Trăm nghe không bằng một thấy" + daily demo.

We don't send weekly status reports. We show you the product — every day.

**Daily demo.** Not a slide deck. Not a Jira board screenshot. The actual product, running, with real data. You click through it. You find the bugs. You tell us "this isn't what I meant" — and we fix it before tomorrow.

Why this matters:
- **No misunderstandings survive more than 24 hours.** If we're building the wrong thing, we find out today, not next sprint.
- **You find the 80/20.** By using the product daily, you naturally discover which 20% of features deliver 80% of the value. We focus there.
- **Quality is built in, not tested in.** Our team uses the product as users before it reaches QA. Every feature has been tested dozens of times by the people who built it — before formal testing even begins.

## Section 5: Your CTO, not your vendor

> 📝 Nội bộ: CTO-as-a-Service mindset — điểm khác biệt cuối cùng.

The most important thing about working with Cyberk isn't our code. It's our conversations.

We don't take your requirements and execute blindly. We challenge your assumptions:

- *"You don't need a blockchain for this."*
- *"This tokenomics model will create sell pressure in month three."*
- *"Your competitor already tried this approach — here's why it failed."*
- *"Spend $5K on product design first. If the idea validates, then spend $35K on development."*

We act as your technology partner — advising on architecture, challenging feature priorities, and helping you avoid the mistakes we've seen across 40+ projects.

That's not a service add-on. It's how we work on every project.

### Tone & style

- Sentence case
- First person plural ("we") — intimate, not corporate
- Conversational — like the CEO is explaining to a potential partner over coffee
- Mỗi section mở bằng insight hoặc contradiction, không bằng definition
- Keep Vietnamese terms (Tâm, Cầu, Nghệ, Tín) — they're unique, memorable, and authentic
