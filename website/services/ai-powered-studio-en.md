# AI-Powered Studio

for Product Solution for Solo-Founders / CEOs

You have a business idea and market development capability (CEO). You need a professional Tech Team, but you don't want to bet on operational risks, expensive hiring costs, and you absolutely hate the "dev lock-in" when a developer leaves and takes all the project knowledge with them.

**AI-Powered Studio** is Cyberk's solution. We provide a 100% online "Engineering Department," combining the structural capacity of an expert with the incredible speed of AI. You give the command, the factory works, and the returned result is an Enterprise-grade product.

In this studio model, we clearly define how the system operates:

## 1. You are the CEO (The Problem Setter)

You just need to provide the business problem, product orientation or create tickets requesting new features, logic changes, or reporting incidents. You hold the role of setting the direction — just focus on user growth and revenue. The technical backend will be handled by the machine and automated systems.

## 2. The Context Engineer is the CTO (Senior Management)

Machines run very fast, but they need someone to steer so they don't deviate from the original architecture. Accompanying the platform is a Context Engineer from Cyberk, acting as your project's CTO:

- **Design & Breakdown:** Receives ideas from the CEO, then designs the context (context-design), turning them into engineering specification languages (Specs).
- **Management & Coordination:** Acts as the prompter, assigning tasks and monitoring the AI workforce (Agents) below. You can go completely "hands-off", or you can be "hands-on" to fine-tune features yourself.
- **Risk Interception:** For extremely complex architectures or massive features, the "CTO" will intercept, maintaining the architectural design instead of letting automation create garbage and hallucination.

## 3. Asimov Pipeline is the Execution Team (Agents)

Under the CTO's command is the Asimov Pipeline — a dedicated AI workforce running online 24/7, executing numerous tasks without complaint:

- **Solution Agent:** Grasps Specs to provide optimal code deployment solutions.
- **Code Agent:** Types source code at lightning speed based on Test-Driven Development (TDD).
- **Review Agent:** 4 AI inspectors execute strict cross-reviews at 4 checkpoints (Logic, Data Security, Contracts, UI) before letting it pass.
- **QA & Manage Agent:** Automated end-to-end testing to prevent bugs before handing it back to the CEO.

The result? Clean source code, no technical debt, ready to scale when the user base explodes.

## 4. Transparent Dashboard (Observability)

Even though you delegate tasks to the CTO and AI, you will never be left in the dark (black-box). The entire factory's operations are transparent and right in front of you:

- **Automatic Reporting (Daily Reports):** Every day, the Agents will automatically summarize and send reports on the tickets they have successfully resolved. You can grasp the work pace without needing long, tedious status update meetings.
- **AI Thought Inspection (Real-time Observability):** At any time, you can open the system to check exactly what task a specific Agent is currently working on. You can even read exactly what they are "thinking" (chain-of-thought and logs) before they decide to type out a single line of code.

## Cost Structure (Subscription)

Hiring a powerful "Tech Team" has never been so transparent in cost:

- **$5,000 / month:** Own the full speed and capacity of the AI Agents team + 01 Context Engineer (acting as a full-time operational CTO).
- **Optional Department (Manual QA): +$3,000 / month:** Add 01 QA Engineer (Human review task). This employee will act as a real user, operate the quality assurance process, and directly reproduce and validate bug reports from your customers.

**AI-Powered Studio: You handle Business. We handle Tech.**
