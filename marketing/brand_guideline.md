# Cyberk Brand Guideline

## Brand Narrative

Jon and Anderson, the founders of Cyberk, met through their mutual friend Logan in 2021, instantly connecting over their shared expertise and boundless passion for technology and software engineering. United by a bold vision, they set out to build a strong, trustworthy startup community across Asia.

Their journey began with a simple but powerful philosophy: "Together." By joining forces, they believed they could spark real breakthroughs—collaborating, growing, and creating lasting value as one. With Vietnam rising as a powerhouse in the global tech scene, Jon and Anderson saw the opportunity to lead the charge in the decentralized future.

They've lived the startup grind—the sprint to MVP, the talent acquisition bottleneck, the need for technical partners who speak your language. Cyberk exists to empower ambitious startups and businesses with rapid, reliable, and innovative Web3 solutions—turning bold ideas into reality and helping dreams thrive in the digital age.

## Brand Vision

At Cyberk, our vision is to be the world's top Web3 technology partner. We exist to make ideas and dreams come true for entrepreneurs and startups, providing rapid, reliable, and innovative Web3 development services that help businesses thrive in the digital economy.

Our mission is "Creating Breakthroughs" - we exist to create real breakthroughs for our clients and the community. In a rapidly changing VUCA world (volatility, uncertainty, complexity, ambiguity), we constantly adapt and quickly master the latest technologies, ensuring we stay at the forefront of innovation and deliver cutting-edge solutions that drive real value for our clients.

We strive to be more than just developers—we aim to be trusted experts who stay on top of the latest Web3 technologies and market trends. With deep technical expertise and industry knowledge, we help our clients build the right solutions and succeed in the fast-moving digital economy.

We navigate the entire product lifecycle, ensuring excellence in every phase—from concept to launch and beyond. This dedication is why our clients return with bigger projects, confident we’ll continue to exceed their high expectations.

## Brand Personality

Cyberk’s brand personality is shaped by our five core values:

**Curiosity**  
We are always eager to learn, open to new ideas, and willing to listen to different perspectives. We seek opportunities to grow and respect those who came before us.

**Craftsmanship**  
We pursue excellence in every detail, striving to perfect our skills and deliver refined, high-quality products. We believe that even the smallest tasks deserve our full attention.

**Integrity**  
We are true to our word and our commitments. We only promise what we can deliver, and we build trust through reliability and honesty.

**Dedication**  
We work wholeheartedly, putting the interests of our clients and team above our own. We empathize with others’ challenges and celebrate our clients’ success as our own.

**Courage**  
We face challenges head-on, embrace difficult tasks, and are willing to take the hard path to do what’s right. We are not afraid of failure, take responsibility, and lead innovation.
