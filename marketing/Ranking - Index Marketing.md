## 2. Hệ Thống Thu Thập Đánh Giá (Review System)

### 2.1. Email Template Yêu Cầu Review

**Subject:** "Quick favor - Your feedback would mean a lot to us!"

**Body:**
```
Hi [Client Name],

Hope you're doing great! We're so grateful for the opportunity to work with you on [Project Name].

We're working on improving our services and would love to get your honest feedback. Would you mind taking 2 minutes to share your experience working with Cyberk?

Here's the direct link: [Clutch/GoodFirms Review Link]

The form asks about:
- Project management & communication
- Quality of deliverables  
- Value for money
- Overall satisfaction

If you'd like, I can jump on a quick 10-minute call to help you structure your thoughts before you write the review. Just let me know!

Your feedback helps other founders find the right tech partner for their Web3 projects.

Thanks so much!
[Your Name]
Cyberk Team
```

### 2.2. Quy Trình Thu Thập Review

**Timeline:**
- **Day 1:** Project completion/delivery
- **Day 3:** Send thank you email + project summary
- **Day 7:** Send review request email
- **Day 10:** Follow-up call/email if no response
- **Day 14:** Final follow-up


### 2.3. Hướng Dẫn Review Chất Lượng

**Gợi ý cho khách hàng:**
- Mention specific metrics (delivery time, cost savings)
- Highlight communication and project management
- Describe the final product quality
- Include any challenges and how we solved them
- Rate all categories (5 stars preferred)
