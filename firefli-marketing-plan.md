## 2) Growth & Community: Testnet + Airdrop Engine

### Participation Program (Zealy/Galxe/Layer3)
- Launch quests with XP/points mirroring on-chain actions and community contributions.
- Bind wallet + socials to reduce Sybil risk; per-wallet rate limits.

### Quest Tracks (score guidance)
- Onboarding: Join Discord (5), join Telegram (5), follow X (5), Warpcast follow (3).
- Renter Track: Create account (10), fund with $BERA (10), mint/receive $tFIRE (15), start a GPU job (inference) (40), finish job + feedback (20), share results on X (10).
- Host Track: Apply as host (10), install client (30), pass health checks (30), stay online 24/48/72h (15/20/25), complete N jobs (per job 10, capped weekly), performance bonus (uptime/success ≥ 98%) (25).
- Ecosystem: Refer renter/host (referral tracked) (15 each), weekly community call attendance (5), thoughtful forum post/tutorial (10–25), bug report with repro (10–30), PR/docs contribution (15–40).

### Anti-Sybil & Quality
- Unique device + basic fingerprint, Discord role gating, wallet history heuristics, CAPTCHA.
- Weighted points for high-friction actions (hosting, successful job completion).

### Referral System
- Personal referral links (UTM + referral code) mapping to wallet/Discord handle.
- Leaderboard (weekly + cumulative); spotlight top referrers in Discord/Twitter.

### Community Ops Cadence
- Weekly community call (demo + roadmap + Q&A) with recordings on YouTube/TG.
- Office hours (hosts/renters) 2× weekly; ticketing via Discord threads/forms.
- AMAs with Berachain/DePIN partners; cross-post to X/TG/DC.
- Moderation SOP: response SLAs, escalation, ban/timeout policies.

## 4) KOL/Partner Marketing

### Target Segments
- Crypto/DePIN KOLs, AI infra builders, GPU homelab creators, Berachain natives, Filecoin community, AI indie devs.

### Tiers & Deliverables (guide)
- Tier A (200k–1M+ reach): 1 dedicated thread + 1 mention + space co-host. Include tracked link + CTA to quests. Budget TBD.
- Tier B (50k–200k): 1 thread or video + 2 tweets + Discord AMA.
- Tier C (5k–50k, niche/dev): tutorial video or blog, community walk-through, GitHub star drive.

### Process
1) Build longlist (50–100) with profiles, relevance, past DePIN/AI content.
2) Outreach with brief + angle + custom hook; offer exclusive early access or codes.
3) Creative approval: ensure claims accurate; include UTMs and unique referral.
4) Track: impressions, CTR, joins, verified quests, jobs started, hosts onboarded.
5) Pay on milestone (content posted + X conversions), not impressions only.

### Co-Marketing
- DePINX co-announcements, joint spaces, blog cross-posts, email blasts.
- Berachain ecosystem accounts, indexers, infra partners; hackathon tie-ins.
